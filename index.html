About Sri Lanka Cricket Club

Founded in 2002, Sri Lanka Cricket Club (SLCC) is one of the oldest and most active Sri Lankan cricket communities in Austria. Our club was established with the vision of bringing together Sri Lankans living in Austria through the shared passion for cricket.


For over two decades, SLCC has promoted sportsmanship, teamwork, and unity while preserving Sri Lankan cricket culture abroad. We provide a platform for players of all skill levels to compete, grow, and build lasting friendships.


Beyond cricket, our club strengthens the Sri Lankan community in Austria through social events, tournaments, and cultural gatherings. We believe in playing as one team, respecting one another, and representing Sri Lanka with pride.
